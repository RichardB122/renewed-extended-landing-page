What's Inside

Current Features

The mod is in active development. Every update expands the world of Middle-earth.

🏰

Factions & Structures

All 6 factions have naturally generating structures, including many brand-new ones not seen in the base mod.

🧙

NPC Hiring

Hire NPCs from Bree, Blue Mountains, Gundabad, Rangers, and High Elves to aid your journey.

⚖️

Full Trade System

The entire trade system has been ported and is fully functional across all faction traders.

🧭

Wandering Traders

5 wandering traders roam the world: Elves, Dwarves, Oddment Collectors, and a new Bree-land Merchant.

🛡️

Banner Protection

Full banner protection ported and ready — especially useful for multiplayer servers.

🌳

New Flora & Items

4 new trees (Chestnut, Willow, Banana, Plum), plus most Legacy blocks and items ported over.
